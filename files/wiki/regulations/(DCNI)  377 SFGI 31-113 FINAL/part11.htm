1.3. System Purpose.


1.3.1. The purpose of the RTES is to:


1.3.1.1. Provide final denial fire before hostile unauthorized access can be gained to controlled/restricted/limited/exclusion areas.


1.3.1.2. Achieve area denial fire of likely avenues of approach to controlled/restricted/limited/exclusion areas.


1.3.1.3. Mitigate identified vulnerabilities that require posting of armed SF IAW (DoD S-5210.41-M_AFMAN31-108V1, Nuclear Weapons Security Manual, Enclosure 6 Para 3.d.(4).)
