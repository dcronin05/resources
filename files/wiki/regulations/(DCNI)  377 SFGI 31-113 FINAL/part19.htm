2.7. 377 SFG/Security Support Squadron/CA will:


2.7.1. Conduct maintenance on the M240B machine guns in accordance with the applicable technical order and commercial manuals.


2.7.2. Change out M240B machine guns as necessary. Change out the M240B drive spring for each weapon at least quarterly in conjunction with a routine inspection.


2.7.3. (FOUO) Zero all M240B machine guns at a full distance range from 350 meters prior to the weapon system being mounted.


2.7.4. Respond upon request from CTS personnel in the event a M240B machine gun needs to be unloaded, loaded, or the weapon itself is damaged during work orders.


2.7.5. Clear expended casings and links from all RTES platforms prior to the RTES Operator closing clamshells after firing the weapon system.
