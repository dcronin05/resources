2.6. Flight Chief/Flight Commander will:


2.6.1. Become familiar with the requirements of AFI 31-101, Integrated Defense (FOUO), AFI 31-117, 377 SFG Tactical Assault Guide (TAG), AFMAN 31-108, Nuclear Weapon Security Manual, and applicable supplements.


2.6.2. Evaluate the operators on their use of the RTES system, knowledge of local procedures, and response to hostile situations by conducting area exercises. (Ensure flight RTES certified operators are given opportunity to use the RTES Simulator to maintain skills.).
