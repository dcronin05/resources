6.4. Work order Tracking Procedures.


6.4.1. The SCS will notify ESS of any work orders opened/closed during the course of duty day. Priority 2 and 3 work orders opened or closed during the no-duty hours will be reported to the ESS no later than the next duty day. Priority 1 work orders opened or closed during no-duty hours will be reported to ESS and CA standby personnel immediately.
