6.3. Closing Work Orders.


6.3.1. The RTES operator enters the names of the persons completing the work and the corrective action on the AFTO Form 781a. The RTES operator will notify SCS when the work has been completed, identify the names of the persons who completed the work and the corrective actions taken.


6.3.2. The SCS will notify the AS, FC/FCC and ESS personnel that the work order has been closed.

6.3.3. The SCS will enter the names of the persons making corrective action and the corrective action(s) taken in the SF Blotter.
