2.8. Electronic Security Systems/Cyber Transport Systems.


2.8.1. ESS will act as System Administrators for the RTES.


2.8.2. Conduct sensor testing of all alarms associated with RTES in accordance with DoD S-5210.41-M-V2_AFMAN 31-108, Nuclear Weapon Security Manual, V2 Enclosure 3.


2.8.3. Respond upon request from CTS personnel in the event a M240B machine gun needs to be unloaded, loaded, or the weapon itself is damaged during work orders.


2.8.4. If a failure of the system affects the platform or the remote electronics, CTS will notify the standby CA personnel.
