3.7. Locating Threat.


3.7.1. To locate the threat from the platform, press the “platform icon,” or swipe 50% or more of the “platform monitor.” The weapon and cameras will begin to move and aim at that platform’s Primary Direction of Fire (PDF). Each tower is assigned its own PDF by programmed default.


3.7.2. The RTES operator will notify the AS which tower is currently selected by calling out “WEAPONS PLATFORM X UP” over the radio. This allows the AS to know the status of the RTES at all times and deploy appropriately.


3.7.3. Track and acquire targets by viewing the monitor to the right of the touch screen and utilize controls to verify target on the main screen.
