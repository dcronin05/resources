2.2. Security Controller Supervisor will:


2.2.1. Act as the SEDS Panel Operator located at SCS Console.


2.2.2. Monitor the RTES operator when the system is enabled/disabled.


2.2.3. Annotate all work orders in the SF Blotter. Additionally, enter the names of the persons making the corrective action and the corrective action(s).


2.2.4. The SCS will notify ESS and CTS of any work orders opened/closed. ESS/CTS response times are outlined in Attachment 9 located in 377 WSSS SOP 31-101, Vol 4. KUMMSC AECS.


2.2.5. Notify the AS, FC/FCC when a work order has been opened/closed.
